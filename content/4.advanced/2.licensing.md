# Licensing Guide

Complete guide to AirShare's licensing system, including Free tier, Pro features, and license management.

## License Tiers

### Free Tier

AirShare is **free to use** with the following limits:

#### Features
- ✅ Cross-platform file sharing

- ✅ Unlimited number of devices
- ✅ Transfer history
- ✅ All languages supported
- ✅ Dark mode and themes
- ✅ Native notifications

#### Limitations
- ⚠️ **200 MB maximum file size** per transfer
- ⚠️ **10 transfers per day** (resets at midnight local time)

## License Activation

### Purchasing a License

1. Visit [airsharehq.github.io](https://airsharehq.github.io)
2. Choose your license type:
   - Pro (1-Year)
   - Pro (Lifetime)
3. Complete purchase
4. Receive license key via email

### Activating Your License

Once you have your license key:

1. Open AirShare
2. Go to **Settings** → **License**
3. Click **"Enter License Key"**
4. Paste your license key
5. Click **"Activate"**

::u-callout
---
icon: i-lucide-check-circle
color: green
---
**Instant activation**: Pro features unlock immediately - no restart required!
::

### License Key Format

License keys look like this:
```
AIRSHARE-PRO-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX
```

- 4 groups of 4 characters
- Case-insensitive
- Copy-paste the entire key
- Don't add spaces or line breaks

### Activation Issues

#### "Invalid License Key"

**Possible causes**:

1. **Typo in key**
   - Copy-paste the entire key from email
   - Don't type manually
   - Remove any accidental spaces

2. **Key already activated**
   - Each key works on one device only
   - Deactivate on other device first
   - Or purchase additional license

3. **Expired key** (1-year licenses)
   - Check expiration date in purchase email
   - Renew or upgrade to lifetime

4. **No internet connection**
   - License activation requires internet
   - Connect to internet temporarily
   - Only needed for activation, not regular use

**Still having issues?** Contact support@airshare.com with:
- Your license key
- Error message
- Operating system

## Managing Your License

### Viewing License Information

Check your license status:

1. Settings → License
2. View:
   - **License type**: Free, Pro (1-Year), or Pro (Lifetime)
   - **Licensed to**: Name on license
   - **Activation date**: When you activated
   - **Expiration date**: For 1-year licenses
   - **Device ID**: Unique identifier for this device

### Deactivating a License

To move your license to another device:

1. Settings → License
2. Click **"Deactivate License"**
3. Confirm deactivation
4. License is freed for use on another device
5. This device reverts to Free tier

::u-callout
---
icon: i-lucide-alert-triangle
color: amber
---
**One device at a time**: Each license can only be active on one device simultaneously.
::

### Transferring a License

To use your license on a different device:

1. **On old device**: Deactivate the license
2. **Install AirShare** on new device
3. **On new device**: Activate with your license key

Your license can be transferred unlimited times, but only one device can be activated at once.

## License Renewal

### 1-Year License Expiration

When your 1-year Pro license expires:

1. **30 days before**: Renewal reminder email
2. **7 days before**: Final reminder
3. **On expiration**:
   - Revert to Free tier (200 MB, 10 transfers/day)
   - All other features still work
   - History is preserved
   - No data is lost

### Renewing

To renew your 1-year license:

**Option 1: Automatic Renewal**
- Enable auto-renewal during purchase
- Charges automatically before expiration
- No interruption of service

**Option 2: Manual Renewal**
- Visit [airsharehq.github.io](https://airsharehq.github.io)
- Purchase new 1-year license
- Or upgrade to Lifetime
- Activate new key when ready

### Grace Period

**7-day grace period** after expiration:
- Keep using Pro features for 7 days
- Renew without reverting to Free
- After 7 days, revert to Free tier

## License Verification

### How License Checks Work

**During activation**:
1. License key sent to activation server (HTTPS)
2. Server validates key
3. Returns license details
4. License stored locally (encrypted)

**After activation**:
- No internet required for daily use
- License verified locally
- Periodic check (every 30 days) for validity
- Works offline indefinitely if already activated

**Privacy**: Only license key and device ID are sent. No usage data or personal information.

## License FAQ

### Can I share my license with someone else?

No. Licenses are:
- Non-transferable between people
- Valid for one device per license
- Personal or business use only

### What happens if I reinstall AirShare?

Your license persists through reinstalls:
- License info stored separately from app
- Reinstalling doesn't affect license
- May need to reactivate if you cleared app data

### Can I use my license on a virtual machine?

Yes, but:
- VM counts as a separate device
- Needs its own license
- Can't share license between host and VM

### Does my license work on all platforms?

Yes! One license works on:
- ✅ Any operating system (Windows, macOS, Linux)
- ✅ But only on one device at a time

### What if I lose my license key?

Email support@airshare.com with:
- Email used for purchase
- Approximate purchase date
- Any order/receipt numbers

We'll resend your license key.

### Can I deactivate and reactivate repeatedly?

Yes! No limit on transfers between devices, but:
- Only one device active at a time
- Don't abuse (automation detection)
